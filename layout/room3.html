Welcome to the Orion Nebula!

Here you will able to buy purple cloud candy -like candy floss but cooler- and faerie dust -what dreams are made of.

All these items are available from local purple shops -the Orion equivalent of a mall.

This nebula may seem like a very secure place, but beware of the pixies, who trade on the large black market. They might offer rare goods but their prices can vary a lot, and many rich traders have come back from Orion emptyhanded after being lured into deals by the pixies.

To travel to another galaxy, go left, right or choose one of the awesome locations at the bottom of the page
